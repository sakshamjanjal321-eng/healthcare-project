Daily meal plan

A full day of balanced, nutritious eating

🌅

Breakfast — 7:00 AM

Start your day with energy-rich, low-sugar foods to kickstart your metabolism.

  • Oats with banana and honey
  • 2 boiled eggs or Greek yogurt
  • Warm lemon water
  • Handful of mixed nuts
~450 kcal
☀️

Lunch — 12:30 PM

A balanced midday meal with protein, complex carbs, and plenty of vegetables.

  • Brown rice or whole wheat roti
  • Grilled chicken or dal
  • Mixed vegetable sabzi
  • Cucumber & tomato salad
~650 kcal
🌤️

Snack — 4:00 PM

A light snack to keep energy steady between lunch and dinner.

  • Fresh fruit — apple or banana
  • Green tea or buttermilk
  • Sprouts chaat or roasted chana
~200 kcal
🌙

Dinner — 7:30 PM

A light, easily digestible dinner that supports overnight recovery.

  • Khichdi or vegetable soup
  • Grilled fish or paneer
  • Steamed broccoli or spinach
  • Warm milk before bed
~500 kcal

Foods to eat & avoid

Simple swaps that make a real difference to your health

✅ Eat More Of These
  • 🥦 Green leafy vegetables
  • 🍎 Fresh seasonal fruits
  • 🥚 Eggs & lean proteins
  • 🌾 Whole grains & millets
  • 🫘 Legumes & lentils
  • 🥛 Low-fat dairy
  • 🫒 Olive oil & healthy fats
  • 💧 Water — 8+ glasses daily
❌ Limit These Foods
  • 🍟 Fried & fast food
  • 🍭 Sugary sweets & sodas
  • 🍞 Refined white bread & pasta
  • 🧂 Excess salt & packaged snacks
  • 🧁 Ultra-processed biscuits & cakes
  • 🍺 Alcohol
  • 🧈 Trans fats & margarine
  • 🥤 Packaged fruit juices

Diet tips

Easy habits to build a healthier relationship with food

🍽️ Eat smaller portions 5–6 times a day to keep metabolism active
🕐 Never skip breakfast — it sets the tone for your whole day's energy
🥗 Fill half your plate with vegetables at every main meal
💧 Drink a glass of water 30 minutes before every meal
🚫 Avoid heavy meals within 2 hours of going to bed
📱 Log what you eat — awareness is the first step to better choices
