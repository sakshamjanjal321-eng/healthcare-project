Wellness areas

Four key pillars of a truly healthy life

🧠

Mental Health

Mental well-being is as important as physical health. Build these habits daily for a calmer, clearer mind.

  • 10 minutes of mindfulness meditation
  • Journaling thoughts before bed
  • Limit screen time to 2 hrs/day
  • Connect with family and friends
  • Take breaks every 90 minutes of work
😴

Sleep Health

Quality sleep is the foundation of recovery, focus, and long-term health. Improve yours tonight.

  • Aim for 7–9 hours every night
  • Sleep and wake at consistent times
  • Avoid caffeine after 3:00 PM
  • Keep your room dark and cool
  • No phone screens 30 min before bed
💧

Hydration

Proper hydration keeps every organ functioning well and energy levels high throughout the day.

  • Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily
  • Start the morning with warm water
  • Drink water before every meal
  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Eat water-rich fruits like watermelon
🧬

Preventive Care

Stay ahead of illness with regular check-ups and smart everyday habits that keep you protected.

  • Annual full body health check-up
  • Dental check every 6 months
  • Eye check-up once a year
  • Blood sugar & cholesterol tests
  • Keep vaccinations up to date

Daily wellness tips

Small daily choices that add up to big health improvements

🌞 Get 15–20 minutes of natural sunlight every morning for vitamin D and mood
🚶 Aim for 7,000–10,000 steps daily — even a short walk after meals counts
🧘 Practice deep belly breathing for 5 minutes when you feel stressed
😄 Laughter genuinely reduces stress hormones and boosts immunity
🥗 Eat a variety of colourful vegetables every day for a wide range of nutrients
📅 Schedule your annual health check-up — prevention is always better than cure
