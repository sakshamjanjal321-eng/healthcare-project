Fitness & Exercise

Move more. Feel stronger. Live better.

Whether you're just starting out or training hard, find a workout plan that fits your body, your goals, and your schedule.

Workout categories

Choose a training style that works for you

💪

Strength Training

Build muscle and power with resistance exercises. Suitable for all fitness levels with progressive overload.

  • Push-ups — 3 sets × 15 reps
  • Squats — 3 sets × 20 reps
  • Dumbbell rows — 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Plank hold — 3 × 45 seconds
🏃

Cardio Training

Improve heart health, burn calories, and boost endurance. Great for weight loss and cardiovascular fitness.

  • Morning run — 30 minutes
  • Jump rope — 15 minutes
  • Cycling — 45 minutes
  • Swimming — 30 minutes
🧘

Flexibility & Yoga

Increase range of motion, reduce injury risk, and improve posture with stretching and yoga flows.

  • Sun salutation — 10 rounds
  • Hamstring stretch — 3 × 30 sec
  • Cat-cow pose — 2 minutes
  • Child's pose — 1 minute
⚡

HIIT Workouts

High-Intensity Interval Training for maximum calorie burn in minimum time — perfect for busy days.

  • Burpees — 4 sets × 10 reps
  • Mountain climbers — 30 seconds
  • High knees — 30 seconds
  • Box jumps — 3 sets × 8 reps

Fitness tips

Small habits that make a big difference

💧 Stay hydrated — drink at least 8 glasses of water daily, and more on training days
😴 Get 7–9 hours of sleep every night for optimal muscle recovery and energy
🔥 Always warm up for 5–10 minutes before jumping into your workout
📈 Track your progress weekly — consistency over perfection is the real secret
🥗 Eat protein within 30 minutes post-workout to support muscle repair
🧘 Cool down and stretch after every session to reduce soreness and improve flexibility
